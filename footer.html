Footer

Footer's remain at the bottom of the page and is home to links to important pages within the site, as well as social media buttons, a logo and copyright notice.

Overview

simplePattern's footer is responsive and simple, taking up the full width of the viewport. It's split into three columns; one for site links, another for social media links and another for the websites logo. Below the three columns is the standard copyright notice.
